Understanding AI Maturity

💡 The 5 Maturity Levels
  • Level 1 - Aware (0-20%): Understanding AI exists but no concrete plans or activities
  • Level 2 - Exploring (21-40%): Researching AI, running experiments, building foundational knowledge
  • Level 3 - Piloting (41-60%): Active pilots showing value, building capabilities systematically
  • Level 4 - Scaling (61-80%): Proven AI in production, expanding across departments, measurable ROI
  • Level 5 - Leading (81-100%): AI embedded in core operations, competitive differentiation, continuous innovation

Assessment Instructions

This assessment evaluates your organization across 6 dimensions of AI maturity. For each question, select the option that best describes your current state. Be honest—this assessment is most valuable when it reflects reality, not aspirations.

The 6 Dimensions:

  1. Strategy & Vision - Clarity and alignment on AI goals
  2. Data & Infrastructure - Foundational capabilities for AI
  3. Talent & Skills - People capabilities and organizational learning
  4. Governance & Ethics - Responsible AI practices and risk management
  5. Implementation & Execution - Ability to deploy and scale AI
  6. Culture & Adoption - Organizational readiness for change
