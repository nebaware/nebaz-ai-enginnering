About Nebaz AI Academy

Nebaz AI Academy is an independent learning platform created by Nebaz to teach AI engineering with Ethiopia at the center — local datasets, real constraints (connectivity, cost in ETB), and career paths that matter in Addis Ababa and beyond.

What makes this different

  • Bootcamp+ curriculum — aligned with 2025–2026 industry practice: RAG evals, agents, MLOps, guardrails, and modern Python tooling.
  • Realtime niche updates — live AI trends from GitHub, arXiv, and Hugging Face with offline fallbacks.
  • Learn anywhere — works offline with local progress and certificates; optional AI tutor tiers when you enable them.

Founder

Nebaz built this academy from the ground up for Ethiopian learners: rearranged courses, new visual identity (green, gold, teal — not generic blue tutorial clones), removed affiliate tool funnels, and added Bootcamp+ modules (RAG evals, agents, MLOps, local project briefs).
