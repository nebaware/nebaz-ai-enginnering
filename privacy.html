Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Local storage

By default, progress, bookmarks, theme, and certificate data are stored in your browser (localStorage) on your device. This data is not sent to a server unless you use hosted features with an explicit backend.

AI tutor

If you enable Ollama or cloud AI tiers, prompts may be sent to the endpoint you configure (localhost or your API proxy). Do not submit secrets or personal data you would not share with that service.

Analytics

Monetization and analytics modules are disabled by default. If enabled later, this page will be updated.
